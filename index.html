VTS — Free Rides to Your VA Appointments

The Veterans Transportation Service (VTS) provides free scheduled rides to and from your VA medical appointments.

Who can use VTS?
  • Any Veteran enrolled in VA health care
  • Scheduled appointments at VA facilities
  • Community care (non-VA) appointments authorized by VA
  • Family caregivers traveling with an eligible Veteran
VTS is available regardless of whether you qualify for Beneficiary Travel mileage reimbursement. You do not need to be BT eligible to ride VTS.
How to schedule a VTS ride
1
Call 509-434-7517 or ask your care team to request transport
2
Provide your name, appointment date and time, location, and any special needs (wheelchair, oxygen, service animal)
3
VTS will confirm your pickup time — schedule as early as possible
4
Your driver takes you door-to-door and returns you home after your appointment
Community care appointments

VTS can also take you to VA-authorized community care (non-VA) appointments. Let us know it is a community care appointment when you call so we can confirm authorization.

Rides are not guaranteed same-day. Call ahead — the sooner the better.
Community Care & Travel Reimbursement

If VA sends you to a non-VA provider through Community Care (TriWest), you may be able to get your travel costs reimbursed — if you are BT eligible.

Your VA provider submits a referral to Community Care. Once TriWest issues an authorization, your appointment is VA-covered. Keep your TriWest authorization — you will need it to file a travel claim.

A referral alone is not enough. You need the TriWest authorization number to get your travel reimbursed for community care visits.
How the authorization process works
1
Your VA provider places a community care referral in your chart
2
VA Community Care office reviews and approves the referral
3
TriWest issues an authorization number to you and the provider
4
You attend the appointment with the authorized provider
5
File your travel claim in BTSSS within 30 days — include your TriWest authorization number
Important: If you go to your appointment before TriWest issues the authorization, your travel claim may not be payable. Wait for your authorization when possible.
Need a wheelchair van or ambulance?

If you need a wheelchair van or ambulance (Special Mode Transport) to get to any VA appointment, your VA provider must place an order in advance. Call us at 509-434-7517 before your appointment so we can arrange it.

Are You Eligible for Beneficiary Travel?

Beneficiary Travel (BT) helps cover your transportation costs to get to VA or VA-authorized medical appointments.

You qualify if you meet ANY ONE of these:

  • Traveling for treatment of a service-connected condition (any rating)
  • You have a service-connected rating of 30% or more (for any appointment)
  • Traveling for a scheduled C&P exam
  • You receive a VA pension
  • Your household income is at or below the VA pension rate for your family size
  • Traveling to get a VA-approved service dog
Not sure if you qualify? Call 509-434-7517 — a staff member will help you find out.
What travel costs can be reimbursed?
  • Mileage at $0.415 per mile (current VA rate)
  • Bus, train, or taxi if medically necessary or no other option
  • Tolls, ferry fares, and bridge fees
  • Meals and lodging only if pre-approved by VA before your trip
Meals and lodging must be approved BEFORE your trip. If you book a hotel first and ask VA to reimburse it after, it will be denied. Always call us first.
What is the deductible?
Trip typeDeductible
One-way trip$3.00
Round trip$6.00
Monthly maximum$18.00 (then free)

The deductible may be waived if you receive a VA pension or your income is at or below the VA pension threshold. Ask us at 509-434-7517.

How to File a Travel Claim

The fastest way to file is online using the Beneficiary Travel Self-Service System (BTSSS) — available 24/7.

File online — BTSSS
1
Scan the QR code below or type the web address into your browser
2
Sign in with your Login.gov or ID.me account
3
Select "Create New Claim" and enter your appointment date and location
4
Enter your mileage or upload receipts for other expenses
5
Submit — reimbursement usually direct deposited within 3–5 business days
